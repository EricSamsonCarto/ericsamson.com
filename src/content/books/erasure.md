The first book I have read by Everett, recommended by a friend. Probably my favorite book of the year so far. I definitely missed the majority of the themes wrapped up in this book, but understood the general idea. After reading some analysis online, apparently it parallels [Invisible Man](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/16981.Invisible_Man) by Ralph Ellison, which I have not read yet but have now added it to my list.

There's a novel within this novel, which is fun, as the main character is a writer. It's a dark comedy that may be a reflection on Everett's own writing career.
