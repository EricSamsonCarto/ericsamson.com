Blake Crouch's other books — [Dark Matter](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/27833670-dark-matter), [Recursion](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/42046112-recursion), and [Upgrade](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/59838811-upgrade) — are all top-tier science fiction. I read those before I touched his Wayward Pines trilogy. As soon as I started, I couldn't stop and finished the three in a weekend.

Science fiction books centered around the long-term effects of climate change are all over my reading list this year. This is the first of my foray into that arena. It could be considered more thriller/fantasy than science fiction, but it crosses a few genres. I have heard the show is not very good, so I have no plans on spoiling what was a great read by watching it, at least for now.
