I love the Trinity Alps in northern California and have previously written about them [here](/projects/trinity-alps-snow-cover). Because of that article, I was contacted by [Justin Garwood](https://backcountrypress.com/book-author/justin-garwood/), who asked if I would like to work on some cartography for a book he was writing with [Michael Kaufman](https://www.michaelkauffmann.net/) about the Klamath Mountains. They sent over data on amphibian diversity and boundaries in the area and asked me to make something. My final version made the cut!

Please consider supporting their work and buy the book! <br>
[The Klamath Mountains: A Natural History](https://siskiyoumountainclub.org/products/the-klamath-mountains-natural-history)
